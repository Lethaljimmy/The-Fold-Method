The Framework

Four Steps.
One Reset.

FOLD is a sequential mental reset process designed for real moments — when the spiral starts, when the reaction is coming, when you need to move through something without losing yourself in it.

F
Focus
Only on what you control. Nothing else gets your energy right now.
O
Own
Only what's yours. Your behavior. Your choices. That's where your power lives.
L
Let
Time take care of time. Some things don't need a decision. They need distance.
D
Decide
The meaning. Not the positive spin — the meaning that makes you the author of this moment.

"FOLD won't always change what's happening.
It will always change who you are while it's happening."

— Tyler Brown, Creator of the FOLD Method

Tyler Brown

Tyler Brown is a Program Director, author, and the creator of the FOLD Method — a framework built not in a classroom, but in the field, working with people in crisis every day.

With over 15 years in large-scale shelter operations, Tyler developed FOLD to answer one question: what do you do when everything hits at once and you can't afford to spiral?

He is also the author of The Culture Myth and a former professional basketball player. He lives and works between New York and Philadelphia.
